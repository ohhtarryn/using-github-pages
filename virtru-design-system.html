Design System & Custom Branding

Virtru

Acting Product Manager, Director of Research & Design

+5% ARR Increase
6x Engineering Output
1.5 Years to Launch

The Challenge

Virtru needed a comprehensive design system to support their growing product suite and enable government compliance. Initially, the company wasn't interested in investing in a design system, but a rebrand and the need for accessible components for government projects created urgency.

The Solution

I stepped up to act as the Product Manager for the design system team when the product team was too busy. We built a comprehensive design system on top of Material-UI (MUI), enabling rapid development and consistent user experiences across all products.

Key Features

  • Accessible design system built on MUI foundation
  • Self-serve custom branding functionality
  • Comprehensive component library
  • Design tokens and documentation
  • Government compliance features

My Process

1

Stakeholder Alignment

Built relationships with engineering, design, and product teams to understand requirements and constraints. Conducted coffee chats to understand pain points and priorities.

2

Research & Discovery

Analyzed existing components and patterns across the product suite. Identified gaps and opportunities for standardization and accessibility improvements.

3

Strategic Planning

Created roadmap and prioritized components based on impact vs. effort analysis. Worked with engineering to estimate development time and resource requirements.

4

Cross-Functional Collaboration

Led regular sync meetings between design and engineering teams. Used visual workshops and wireframing to align on component specifications and behavior.

5

Implementation & Launch

Managed the 1.5-year development process, ensuring quality and accessibility standards. Launched with comprehensive documentation and training materials.

Results & Impact

Business Impact

  • 5% ARR increase through custom branding feature
  • Enabled Virtru to move upmarket with enterprise clients
  • Reduced onboarding costs for new customers
  • Improved government compliance and reduced legal risk

Engineering Efficiency

  • 6x increase in engineering output
  • Faster component development and iteration
  • Reduced design-to-development handoff time
  • Consistent code quality across teams

User Experience

  • Consistent design language across all products
  • Improved accessibility for all users
  • Better brand recognition and trust
  • Streamlined user onboarding

Team Impact

  • Improved cross-functional collaboration
  • Clearer design and development processes
  • Reduced design debt and technical debt
  • Better documentation and knowledge sharing

Key Learnings

Start Small, Think Big

Even when the company wasn't initially interested, we started working on the design system slowly. When the rebrand and government compliance needs arose, we were ready to scale quickly.

Cross-Functional Leadership

Acting as PM for a design system required deep collaboration with both design and engineering teams. My UX background helped me understand both perspectives and facilitate better communication.

Business Value First

By tying the design system to business outcomes (custom branding, government compliance), we were able to secure buy-in and demonstrate clear ROI throughout the process.
