My PM Philosophy

Relationships First

The first thing I do when joining a company is start building relationships in my first few weeks with coffee chats. Strong relationships enable better collaboration, faster decision-making, and more effective cross-functional work.

Impact vs. Effort

I like a simple impact vs. effort framework and doing these with both design and engineering in the room so we have a good idea of our estimates. This collaborative approach ensures realistic planning and shared ownership.

Visual Alignment

I drive alignment by workshops and facilitation from my design background. Visualizing flows and sketching wires helps people communicate better as well as asking people for their insights.

Research-Driven Decisions

My background is in UX, which means I don't just ship features, I make sure we're solving the right problems and building things customers will actually use. Research saves time, reduces rework, and keeps teams moving in the same direction.
