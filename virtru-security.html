Director of Research & Design

$2.2M ARR Generated

The Challenge

Virtru needed to launch a new security product from scratch. What started as an executive's pet project required strategic research and product direction to determine what should actually be built versus what was unnecessary.

The Solution

I directed my team to conduct comprehensive research to understand market needs and user requirements. Through this research, we identified that the product needed both inbound and outbound capabilities, which significantly increased our customer acquisition potential.

Key Features

  • Inbound security capabilities
  • Outbound security features
  • User-friendly interface design
  • Scalable architecture
  • Integration with existing Virtru products

My Process

1

Research & Discovery

Conducted extensive market research to understand what customers actually needed versus what was initially planned. This research-driven approach saved significant development time and resources.

2

Strategic Pivoting

Used research insights to pivot from the original executive vision to a more market-driven product strategy that addressed real customer pain points.

3

Cross-Functional Leadership

Led design and research teams to create user-centered solutions while collaborating with engineering to ensure technical feasibility.

4

Product Launch

Managed the end-to-end launch process, ensuring the product met user needs and business objectives while maintaining quality standards.

Results & Impact

Business Impact

  • $2.2M ARR generated from new product
  • Significant revenue growth for Virtru
  • New market segment captured
  • Increased customer acquisition

User Adoption

  • 600K monthly users actively using the product
  • High user engagement and retention
  • Positive user feedback and reviews
  • Strong product-market fit achieved

Strategic Value

  • Research-driven product decisions
  • Both inbound and outbound capabilities
  • Scalable product architecture
  • Foundation for future product development
